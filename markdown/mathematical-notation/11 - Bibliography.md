[11] Scott Pakin. The comprehensive $\mathrm{LaTeX}$ symbol list. http://www.ctan.org/tex-archive/info/symbols/comprehensive/.

[13] Eric Weisstein. *Wolfram Mathworld*. http://mathworld.wolfram.com/.
